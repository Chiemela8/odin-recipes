Brownies

Description

Brownies is a desert that is delicious and everybody loves.

It's simple to make and bake.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Water
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Unsweeted Coco Powder
  • Oil
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Mix Ingredients
  2. Put it in the oven & Bake
  3. Let it Bake at 350 for 25 to 30 minutes
  4. When done, Let it sit for 15
  5. Enjoy
