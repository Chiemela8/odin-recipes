Ramen

Description

Ramen is that one dish you go to when you're poor in college.

Ramen can save lives & it's taste can save yours too.

Ingredients

  • Ramen Packet
  • Seasoning & Spice from packet
  • Few cups of Water

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Put Ramen in Boiled Water
  3. Let it Sit For 10 Minutes
  4. Add Seasoning and Spice
  5. Enjoy
