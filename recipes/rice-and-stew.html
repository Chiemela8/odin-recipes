Rice And Stew

Description

Rice And Stew is a Nigerian dish that is delicious.

It's simple to make and it's one of my favorite dishes.

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups of Rice
  • 20 Fresh Tomatoes
  • 5 Cups of Vegetable Oil
  • 10 Fresh Pepper
  • 2 Onions
  • 3 Tablespoons Curry
  • 7 maggi cube
  • Salt
  • 1 Tablespoon Thyme
  • 3 pieces of Ginger
  • 6 Cloves Garlic
  • 300g Chicken Meat

Steps

  1. Boil The Rice
  2. Get a New Pot and Add Your Vegetable Oil, Let it heat up
  3. Add Onions, peppers, Tomatoes once it's heated
  4. Sir and Allow to Cook
  5. Drain YourBoiled Rice
  6. Add Some Salt
  7. Mix and Servce as Desired
